Local Law 97 & Rent Burden

The Clash of Energy Efficiency and Housing Affordability

This map categorizes census tracts with Local Law 97-covered multifamily housing buildings into four quadrants by their level of rent burden and greenhouse gas emissions.

While LL97 is critical to decarbonizing NYC's housing stock, tracts with both high rent burden and high emissions might be at risk of housing instability if property owners pass on compliance costs to already-burdened tenants.
